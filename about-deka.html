THANDEKA LAKA

Designer, Strategist & User Advocate

I'm Thandeka Laka, a designer who loves vanquishing wicked problems.

I could have been an engineer, a doctor or an attorney, but life had other plans, and honestly, so did I.

Instead, I bet on myself and became a different kind of advocate. Not the courtroom kind, but the kind who defends users in boardrooms, workshops, product rooms and everyday decisions that shape products.

Work & experience

Currently based in Johannesburg, South Africa, working as a Product Design Lead at Accenture Song. Previously, I collaborated with the design team at Absa Group, and earlier in my career I worked in radio and advertising at Primedia Broadcasting.
